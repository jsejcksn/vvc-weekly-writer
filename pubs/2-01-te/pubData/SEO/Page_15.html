The elements of RIP pull from well-known, research-based reading strategies, and put them into an easy process for students of all ages. As you guide students through this lesson, point out that after reading the first two sentences, a red flag should pop up. This sounds and feels like a math lesson. Watch out! Which information is important? In your mind, convert the number words to digits and start predicting what math operation you will have to do. Additionally, take notice of the descriptive language. Is it odd to find descriptive language in a math problem? Wow! Is descriptive language truly all around us? We need to be more observant. Now that we know it’s a math problem, let’s be on the lookout for additional information we may need to solve a potential problem. And, how will we recognize the instructions?
