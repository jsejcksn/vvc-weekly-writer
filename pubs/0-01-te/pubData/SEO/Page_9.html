Our goals are — • Introduce telling sentences • Introduce the period at the end of telling sentences • Introduce capitalizing the first word of a sentence Even though Mr. Butler and RIP introduced these concepts in the video, it is important for you to continue the implementation of these skills into your group writing lessons.
