Virtual Village Classroom © 2015 Virtual Village Classroom © 2015 Virtual Village Classroom © 2015 Virtual Summary In the video, you learned the keyword for descriptive writing is describe. When you see this word in directions, you will know you need to describe. Mr. Butler helped you brainstorm ways to describe a toy. Mr. Butler made a plan for describing. He used bullets as a way to organize the plan. Sentence by sentence, Mr. Butler modeled complete sentences that described the toy. Finally, Mr. Butler revised and edited the paragraph. Your Turn! Using the modeled piece of writing, you should write your own descriptive paragraph about a toy. The toy can be one from your younger years or a toy you play with currently. Be sure to follow the process of brainstorming, planning, writing, and revising and editing as Mr. Butler did during the lesson.
