2015 Virtual Village Classroom © 2015 Virtual Village Classroom © 2015 Virtual Village Classroom © 2015 Virtual Village SUMMARY In the video, Mr. Butler and RIP described an apple. He used a graphic organizer called a web to brainstorm for ideas. Brainstorming is thinking of many, different ideas. Next, Mr. Butler made a plan to write his sentences. As he wrote each sentence, he built it out loud to organize the words so the sentence would make sense. Then, he wrote the words on the board to create complete sentences. Finally, Mr. Butler and RIP read the sentences to look for mistakes. They corrected their mistakes so the reader could read them without any problems. YOUR TURN! Think about a banana! Use the same steps Mr. Butler and RIP used to write about the apple to describe a banana. First, you need to brainstorm. Next, you need to make a plan. Then, you need to write your sentence or sentences. When you are finished, you need to revise and edit for mistakes. Your teacher will guide you through the writing process to create an amazing description of a banana!
